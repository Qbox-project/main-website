Qbox Project

Qbox is an advanced core with a load of bloat. Commonly known as a Framework. Aiming to deliver high quality and industry standard resources. Focused on RolePlay worthy resources that are usable on the CitizenFX platforms. But also usable for other purposes besides RolePlay.

  • Download Now

Why Choose Qbox

Compatibility

QBCore compatibility is provided as a courtesy for an easy transistion. Our goal is to make every resource, escrowed or not, compatible with qbx_core.

Revamped Resources

Qbox isn't just a refactor of the antiquated QBCore resources you've already uninstalled. Refactored and revitalized, every resource in our recipe has been reworked to a higher coding standard.

Lightweight

With a total CPU msec of 0.42 ms and a Memory usage of around 30 MiB Qbox is one of the fastest and lightweight server packs that you can use.

Secure Code

Qbox has been written with security in mind. This way you don't have to worry about cheaters triggering events.
